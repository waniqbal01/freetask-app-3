# Get Billplz Credentials from Render

## Quick Steps

1. **Go to Render Dashboard**: https://dashboard.render.com
2. **Select your service**: `freetask-api`
3. **Click "Environment"** tab on the left
4. **Copy these values**:
   - `BILLPLZ_API_KEY`
   - `BILLPLZ_COLLECTION_ID`
   - `BILLPLZ_X_SIGNATURE`

## Paste the values here for reference:

```
BILLPLZ_API_KEY=
BILLPLZ_COLLECTION_ID=
BILLPLZ_X_SIGNATURE=
```

Then you can copy these to DigitalOcean environment variables.

---
